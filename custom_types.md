# Custom Types — Erova Region

## The Triangle
Cosmic > Spirit > Mystic > Cosmic

Cosmic overwhelms Spirit (celestial transcends earthly life force).
Spirit disrupts Mystic (living essence breaks enchantment).
Mystic binds Cosmic (arcane magic contains celestial power).

## Design Rationale

**Cosmic** — Celestial energy, starlight, the void between worlds.
- Overwhelms mental (Psychic) and shadow (Dark) forces; transcends earthly spirits (Spirit)
- Bounces off material matter (Steel, Ground); mirrors cancel (Cosmic)
- Arcane magic can contain it (Mystic); spectral (Ghost) and toxic (Poison) forces corrupt it

**Mystic** — Arcane magic, runes, enchantment, ancient knowledge.
- Magic overwhelms the mundane (Normal), physical (Fighting), and cosmic (Cosmic)
- Darkness resists enchantment (Dark); cold iron blocks magic (Steel); mirrors cancel (Mystic)
- Living spirit disrupts spells (Spirit); instinct bypasses illusion (Bug); earth grounds magic (Ground)

**Spirit** — Life force, souls, ancestral energy, vitality.
- Life force purifies death (Ghost), overwhelms mind (Psychic), disrupts enchantment (Mystic)
- Corruption resists vitality (Dark); soulless metal blocks it (Steel); mirrors cancel (Spirit)
- Celestial forces transcend earthly life (Cosmic); darkness corrupts (Dark); cold kills (Ice)

## Balance Notes
- All three types have identical profile: 3 SE, 3 NVE, 3 weaknesses, 4-5 resistances, 0 immunities
- Steel resists all three (thematic: cold iron/material blocks magic/spirit/cosmos)
- Dark resists Mystic and Spirit but is weak to Cosmic (cosmic is the one force darkness can't hide from)
- Psychic is weak to both Cosmic and Spirit (mind is vulnerable to forces beyond thought)
