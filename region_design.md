# Region: Erova — Full Design Document

## Overview

Erova is a massive, geographically diverse region built around a central mountain range called **The Spire**. The region is structured as 6 geographic zones arranged in a rough spiral leading toward The Spire, with a 7th zone — the **Floating Islands** — accessible only after conquering The Spire's summit.

The region's culture revolves around **The Circuit** — a legendary trainer challenge that tests trainers across every biome and battle condition Erova has to offer.

## Geographic Zones & Circuits

### Circuit 1: The Verdant Coast (Levels 5-28)
**Biome:** Coastal grasslands, beaches, fishing villages, a lighthouse
**Theme:** Beginnings, the sea, exploration
**Starter Town:** Tidewell Village — a small fishing hamlet where the professor's lab overlooks the ocean

**Towns (linear path):**
1. **Tidewell Village** — Starter town. Professor Coral's lab. Rival lives next door.
2. **Saltbreeze Town** — First gym. Small seaside market town.
3. **Coralridge City** — Second gym. Larger port city with a harbor and shipyard.
4. **Dunecrest Town** — Third gym. Sandy beach town transitioning to grassland.
5. **Mosswall City** — Fourth gym + Mini League 1 arena. Walled city at the coast-forest border.

**Routes:** 5-6 routes with beaches, coastal cliffs, tide pools, grassy meadows
**Gym Types (suggested):**
- Gym 1: Normal (Lv 12-14) — "The Basics"
- Gym 2: Water (Lv 17-19)
- Gym 3: Flying (Lv 22-24)
- Gym 4: Bug (Lv 26-28)

**Mini League 1:** 4 trainers + a Circuit Master. Earn the **Tide Medal**.

**Regional Legendary:** *Coralith* — a Rock/Water ancient coral golem sleeping beneath the reef. Found in the **Sunken Temple** (post-Circuit 1 dungeon).

**Villain Subsidiary:** **AetherFish Industries** — a fishing megacorp draining the coastal ecosystem. They're overharvesting rare water Pokemon and polluting the reef where Coralith sleeps.

### Circuit 2: The Emerald Wilds (Levels 28-48)
**Biome:** Dense forest, ancient ruins, misty glades, a great river
**Theme:** Nature vs. civilization, history, secrets

**Towns:**
1. **Fernhollow Town** — Forest entrance village. Rangers' headquarters.
2. **Thornwick City** — Fifth gym. City built into the trees, connected by bridges.
3. **Gloomveil Town** — Sixth gym. Misty town near ancient ruins.
4. **Rootbridge City** — Seventh gym. City spanning a massive river on a natural stone bridge.

**Routes:** Forest paths, river crossings, ruins, mushroom groves
**Gym Types:**
- Gym 5: Grass (Lv 30-32)

- Gym 7: Poison (Lv 40-42)
- Gym 8: Fairy (Lv 45-48)

**Mini League 2:** Earn the **Verdant Medal**.

**Regional Legendary:** *Sylvanthor* — a Grass/Fairy ancient tree spirit that protects the deep forest. Found in the **Heart Grove** after solving the ruins' puzzles.

**Villain Subsidiary:** **VerdaCorp Logging** — clear-cutting the ancient forest for development. They're disturbing the ruins and weakening the barrier that keeps Sylvanthor dormant.

## The Full Circuit: The Floating Islands (Levels 115-135)

After earning all 6 medals, the summit of The Spire opens to reveal a stairway of floating stone islands ascending into the sky. These are the **Celestial Steps** — ancient battlegrounds where the greatest trainers in Erova's history proved themselves.

### Structure (5-4-3-2-1):

**5 Circuit Gyms** (Lv 115-120):
The five toughest type specialists in Erova, each representing a fundamental element:
1. Earth Gym — Ground/Rock specialist
2. Sea Gym — Water/Ice specialist
3. Sky Gym — Flying/Dragon specialist
4. Mind Gym — Psychic/Ghost specialist
5. Heart Gym — Fairy/Fighting specialist

**4 Elite Four** (Lv 120-125):
Multi-type strategists, each with a unique battle style:
1. **Strategist Kael** — Weather-based team (sets up weather and exploits it)
2. **Warden Mira** — Stall/tank team (walls, recovery, status)
3. **Ace Rhys** — Hyper offense (speed, coverage, glass cannons)
4. **Sage Elara** — Trick Room/gimmick team (reverses expectations)

**3 Guardians** (Lv 125-128):
Ancient trainers who guard the upper islands. Each has a theme:
1. **Guardian of the Past** — Uses fossil/ancient Pokemon + the regional legendaries' pre-evolutions
2. **Guardian of the Present** — Uses the strongest Pokemon from every Circuit
3. **Guardian of the Future** — Uses experimental/unusual strategies, held items, abilities

**2 Champions** (Lv 128-132):
1. **Champion Auren** — The reigning champion. Full balanced team, no weaknesses.
2. **Champion Vesper** — Your rival, who reached the top just before you.

**1 Region Leader** (Lv 132-135):
**Region Leader Orion** — The founder of the Circuit system. Legendary trainer. Full team of Lv 135 Pokemon with perfect IVs, competitive movesets, and held items. The ultimate test.

## The Villain Team: Nexus Corporation

**Nexus Corp** is a massive conglomerate with subsidiaries in every zone. On the surface, they're Erova's largest employer — energy, mining, logging, fishing, biotech. They sponsor Pokemon trainers and fund research.

**The truth:** Nexus Corp's CEO, **Director Haze**, discovered that Erova's 6 regional legendaries are fragments of a single ancient entity called **Primordis** — the Pokemon that created the Erova region itself. By capturing all 6 and bringing them to the summit of The Spire, Nexus Corp plans to reassemble Primordis and harness its power to reshape reality — creating a "perfect world" under their control.

### Story Arc:

**Circuits 1-2:** Player encounters subsidiary operations causing environmental damage. Seems like standard corporate greed. Rival dismisses it as "just business."

**Circuit 3:** Player discovers the subsidiaries are all connected to Nexus Corp. Finds research notes about "Project Convergence." A Nexus Admin becomes a recurring antagonist.

**Circuit 4:** Nexus Corp openly begins capturing regional legendaries. Player races to reach each legendary first. Some are saved, some are captured. The rival starts taking the threat seriously.

**Circuit 5:** Full-scale Nexus Corp invasion. They've captured 4 of 6 legendaries. Player must infiltrate a Nexus base to free them. Director Haze is revealed.

**Circuit 6:** Race up The Spire. Nexus Corp is drilling into the Core Chamber to reach Terravolt. Final confrontation with Director Haze at the summit. Player stops the convergence but Haze escapes.

**Post-Full Circuit:** After becoming Region Leader, player gains access to the **Primordial Sanctum** at the very peak of the Floating Islands. Here, they can encounter **Primordis** (the true final legendary) — a ??? type or a unique dual-type like Normal/Dragon. Primordis acknowledges the player as worthy.

Director Haze makes one last attempt to capture Primordis. Final battle against Haze's team + a corrupted shadow version of Primordis. The player uses the real Primordis to cleanse the corruption. Erova is saved.
