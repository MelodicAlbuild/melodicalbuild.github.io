# Fakemon Design Document — Erova Region

## Overview
- 5 Cosmic evolutionary lines (13 Pokemon)
- 4 Mystic evolutionary lines + 1 single-stage (11 Pokemon)
- 5 Spirit evolutionary lines (13 Pokemon)
- 7 Legendary/Mythical Pokemon
- **Total: 44 new Pokemon**

All BST (Base Stat Total) follows standard Pokemon design:
- Stage 1: 280-320 BST
- Stage 2: 400-440 BST
- Stage 3: 500-535 BST
- 2-stage final: 480-515 BST
- Single stage: 490-510 BST
- Legendaries: 580-680 BST

## CHAMPION NAME

**Champion Auren** — The reigning champion. Name evokes "golden/dawn," contrasting rival Vesper ("evening star"). Auren reached the top first; the player must surpass the dawn itself.
